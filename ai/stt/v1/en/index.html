HOW VIEWERS USE IT

Here is how viewers can use subtitles

When an admin sets AI subtitles to be visible to all viewers, a CC button appears in the player. Viewers can tap the CC button to turn on subtitles, choose their preferred language, and adjust the subtitle position and style to create a viewing experience that suits them.

STEP 01

Tap the 'CC icon' to turn on AI subtitles

For broadcasts where the admin has made AI subtitles public, a CC button appears at the top of the player. Viewers can tap the CC button to turn on subtitles and enter the subtitle settings menu.
