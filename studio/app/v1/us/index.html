Join the Beta

Up and running in 5 minutes.

Free to join. Any iPhone running iOS 15.0 or later is all you need.

1
Tell your CSM you'd like to join

Let your assigned CSM know you want to participate. You'll receive an invitation email and install link. If the email doesn't arrive, check your spam folder first.

2
Open the install link on your iPhone

Open the link on your iPhone — PC, Android, and iPad won't work. A Wi-Fi connection is recommended for the initial download.

3
Install TestFlight (if not already installed)

Tap [Install TestFlight] on the link page and install it for free from the App Store. Skip this step if TestFlight is already on your device.

4
Install Shoplive Studio Beta

After installing TestFlight, you'll be redirected to the Studio page automatically. Tap [Install]. When you see the orange dot (beta badge) on the app icon, installation is complete.

5
Share feedback and discuss full adoption

Send feedback via the in-app button or your CSM channel. We'll use your beta experience to plan a full rollout together.
